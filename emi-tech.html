Before You Begin
What This App Does
EMI-Tech guides your child's eyes through smooth, calming movement patterns based on Eye Movement Integration therapy. The movements help the brain de-escalate during moments of distress. Breathing exercises between patterns promote regulation.
How Vibration Works
The full EMI-Tech device uses vibrating handles that pulse in sync with the visual patterns, providing bilateral tactile stimulation. As the arrow moves left, the left handle vibrates stronger. As it moves right, the right handle takes over. This multi-sensory approach (visual + tactile + audio) engages more neural pathways than visual alone.
Using a Game Controller (Recommended)
Connect a Bluetooth game controller (Xbox, PlayStation, etc.) to simulate the bilateral vibration. The controller's left and right rumble motors will pulse with the arrow movement, just like the real device's handles. Press any button on the controller to connect it.
No controller detected
Use Headphones
A calming tone pans from left ear to right ear following the arrow. With headphones, this creates an auditory version of the bilateral stimulation. This works on all devices including iPhone.
